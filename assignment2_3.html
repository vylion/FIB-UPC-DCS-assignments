We start with a circle of radius R, and we know the parametrization of the circle
is: {x = a+R*cos(t), b+R*sin(t)], where t stands for the steps of drawing ("time"),
and {a,b} is the center.
To this, we add movement along the y-axis: a = R*t. And then, we displace the circle's
center so it rolls over the x-axis.

Parametrization is the following:
x = t*R+R+R*cos(t)
y = R+R*sin(t)
